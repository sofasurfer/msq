ROME TRAVEL CONCIERGE

Travel Like A Local

Going to a new destination, or revisiting a beloved locale, nurtures and fuels my endless interest in how humans navigate their world. It is important to me to inspire and facilitate others to travel deeply, seeking sincere engagement with local context and perspectives. Ultimately, travel, and genuine experiences in other cultures, sparks a more nuanced, profound comprehension of our own.

ABOUT MS Q

I’m just a globe-trotting woman obsessed with travel plus eating & drinking well – from 5 star to street food. Ever Fly Ms Q is a natural expression of my personal zeal for food, cocktails, wine and creating authentic experiences for convivial, like-minded bon vivants. I’m that friend that you call when you want to know what to eat, drink and do in a new destination.

I cut my gastronomic teeth as a concierge for Four Seasons Hotels in Philadelphia & New York City, then moved on to doing events in far flung locales like Singapore and Thailand. I spent a decade traversing South East Asia, but have returned to my original love affair, Rome, Italy, where I am now based (usually cooking alongside my Roman husband)…all roads do indeed lead here, for me.

This is what drives me, enriches my life and provides meaningful interactions: the truism that we are all here seeking the same things in this life: security, joy, pleasure, meaning.
